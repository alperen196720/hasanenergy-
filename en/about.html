About Us

HasanEnergy officially started its commercial operations in March 2018, built upon more than 20 years of industry experience and technical expertise.

Our company provides services in LPG storage systems, LPG cylinder filling and transportation equipment, pressure vessels, mechanical and piping systems, as well as fuel, cryogenic and various industrial applications.

HasanEnergy supports its customers throughout the entire process including project development, manufacturing, installation, commissioning, training and after-sales technical support.

With a strong technical infrastructure and cooperation with leading companies in the sector, we continue to be a reliable solution partner focused on customer satisfaction.

Our goal is to accurately understand customer needs and deliver complete, high-quality and standard-compliant solutions on time.
